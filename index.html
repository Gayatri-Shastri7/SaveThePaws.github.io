About

About

We, the team of Save The Paws, are a group of people working towards the rehabilitation and re-homing of abused and abandoned dogs. We do this by arranging adoption camps at various locations across Mumbai. These adoption camps invite rescuers from all over the city to bring their rescued animals and be able to meet with so many potential adopting families. All the funds that we collect during the adoption camps as registration charges, go towards feeding and rescue of other dogs. So far, we have successfully organized more than 20 adoption campsand have seen more than 300 rescued animals starting a new and better life with their forever families.
